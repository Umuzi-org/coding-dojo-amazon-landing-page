FAQs

How much does the program cost

Your out of pocket expenses are $0 if accepted to the program and you are an eligible employee for Amazon Career Choice programs! Amazon Career Choice covers 95% of tuition and Coding Dojo has waived the remaining 5% to assist you in kick starting your career path in tech. Please note that only eligible employees qualify and you have a laptop that meets our requirements with a strong connection to the internet to participate.

Will Amazon adjust my work schedule so I can attend classes?

Full-time employees should discuss scheduling needs with HR or Workflow. They can correctly adjust your schedule so you can participate in Coding Dojo training.

Am I eligible for this Career Choice program?

Most employees are eligible to apply after one continuous year of employment. If eligible, you can apply up to 90 days before your one-year anniversary! Eligibility requirements may vary based on whether you are part-time, full-time, hourly or salaried.

What credentials will I earn at the end of this program?

Graduates of our Web Development program earn an Certificate of Completion.
